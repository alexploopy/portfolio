This award has a full writeup. Detailed awards use the same front matter as
experiences and projects, plus a Markdown body, and get their own detail page.
Reference one from `content/awards/list.json` with `{ "ref": "featured-award" }`;
simple awards are just `{ "title": "...", "description": "..." }`.
